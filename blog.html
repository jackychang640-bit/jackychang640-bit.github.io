03: doing things

apologies to all 0 of my daily readers for the inactivity. when i initially set up this blog page i anticipated that i'd be posting frequently. here's an update on the cool things i've been up to since october 13th:

playing lots of games! back in february i made a list of things i wanted to achieve by the end of the year. among them was listening to 10 new albums, watching 10 new movies, and playing/beating 10 new games. i've defintely reached the album goal and i'm probably close to the game goal, but i think i've only seen something like 5 movies this year. i'll be sure to post the full list when the time comes.

my fall break started back on the 10th so i've had more opportunities to play different games. but i started using my ps2 for real a few weeks earlier, after i replaced the cmos battery. now the little save icons show the correct dates and times! :) for a few days the only thing i did after coming home from school was playing lumines plus.. that game rocks. even though i've had my ps2 since 2022, i've barely touched any of the games i have for it. it was really fun trying a game out for an hour and then switching it out for another one. i tried out katamari damacy, metal gear solid 2, pac-man world 2, hot shots golf fore, ddr, (which is actually pretty fun even with a controller) jak and daxter, and ridge racer v!! ridge racer v is sooo good that's gotta be one of the most satisfying games to play for real...

this week, however, i've been playing lots of gamecube games. well, i've been playing lots of two specific gamecube games. kirby air ride and animal crossing! it's nice having a smaller library of games to choose from. it's far less daunting than my endless digital library on steam. something i've really been wanting to make on this site since i created the blog section is a subpage called "game log" or something, where i'll talk in more detail about a game i've been playing. it would also tie back to the "play 10 new games this year" thing and help me keep track of said games. anyways, that's probably enough for one blog post. kinda lacked focus, but so what? that's the whole point of this page, and to a greater extent, this whole site! just typing stuff for fun. see you in the next post.

01: cool songs

now that the site's been fully re-written, i thought it'd be fun to share some cool songs i've been listening to lately. keep in mind that i'm not a music reviewer by any means so don't be expecting in-depth write-ups.

Chubby Pitcher56 - Pitcher56 1st Album
after not putting out any new music in over a decade, Pitcher56 came back in 2022 with the (aptly..?) titled 'Pitcher56 1st Album' and this opening track is so good. the piano is my favourite instrument and it sounds great here.

If You Want It Niteflyte - Niteflyte
the guitar in the opening to this one seriously reminded me of 'Sparkle' by Tatsuro Yamashita, one of my all-time favourite songs + artists. if you can find an upload somewhere, i urge you to take a listen. as for 'If You Want It,' the strings are really nice and the vocals are amazing. plus, the hook "if you want it, you can get it, with your love" is super catchy.

Fine Whine (feat. Joe Fox, Future & M.I.A.) A$AP Rocky - AT.LONG.LAST.A$AP
yet again, im a sucker for piano. i adore Rocky's experimental production, especially on his 2018 album 'TESTING.' i must've listened to this song on repeat a dozen times yesterday while drawing.

Bubbles jenny01 - jenny01 Best
this song shares a lot of vibes with 'Chubby.' 1:40 rocks, especially with headphones on. very cheerful-sounding song all around. this is the type of music i'd like to make someday. :)
