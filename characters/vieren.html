bio

vieren is a firefly who works as a lamplighter. it's not the most interesting job in the world, but he likes it. others might find it tedious, but vieren finds comfort in the monotony of his daily routine. with his lamplighter's wick (and ladder,) he brings light to the otherwise dim streets. i guess those wings are just for show, huh?

vieren tends to keep to himself, but that's not entirely by choice. as the sole lamplighter of the region, most of his time is spent on the job. he doesn't seem to mind that much, though. besides, it's led to at least one memorable encounter..

he actually lives in an area that's a bit further away from the rest of my characters. as such, he hasn't really interacted with them.

species....firefly
gender.....male
height.....the tallest
weight.....light
likes......poste
dislikes...interruptions to his work (with one exception)
