bio

pitch is a wasp who, in the past, made his living as a performer. nowadays, he does so as a thief. while these two professions may be worlds apart, the acrobatic skills he developed back then have served his new life quite well. he may seem like a pretty laid-back guy, but with his expertise and his toothpick spear, he's not to be taken lightly.

after leaving his troupe, pitch has stuck to himself. others just get in the way, don't they? too many variables, too many things that could go wrong, especially during a heist. despite this, (plus some initial resistance on pitch's part) he's made an unlikely friendship with a bee named cleft.

since then, not much has changed. to this day, pitch has never been caught, and cleft still doesn't know what he actually does for work...

species....wasp
gender.....male
height.....average (for a bug)
weight.....light
likes......thievery, decluttering
dislikes...when things are out of place
