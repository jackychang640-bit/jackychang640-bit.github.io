bio

lena is an ant who likes hiking, exploring, and crafting all sorts of things. she's got her own shop where she sells various odds and ends she's collected along with some handmade accessories. with the amount of materials she's got piled up, tailored requests are no problem! (like with puffy's cape!)

lena loves making things for her friends. her favourite (and most popular) items incorporate beads that she chisels out of different minerals. they're just ordinary rocks, but they look really nice! she wears accessories made from these beads on her antennae, head, and wrists.

she became friends with poste after a series of consistent orders. sometimes, he sticks around after a delivery to chat or to hear some of lena's advice.

species....ant
gender.....female
height.....average (for a bug)
weight.....average
likes......hiking, bouldering, crafting, all of it!!
dislikes...thieves
