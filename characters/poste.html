bio

poste is a termite mailbug. you might think that an ant would be better suited for the position with their strength, but few can rival the determination poste has for his job. your package will arrive on time, and that's a poste guarantee!

he might come across as timid sometimes, but tries his best to act outgoing. he wants to set a good example for the post office, after all.

poste lacks strength and is a bit clumsy, but makes up for it with his encyclopedic knowledge of addresses and routes. he's a pro at calculating the quickest path from point a to point b! (permitting that he's actually familiar with the area..)

he's familiar with lena, as she's a frequent customer, and vieren, whom he met while making a distant delivery...

species....termite
gender.....male
height.....second tallest
weight.....light
likes:.....consistency
dislikes...being startled
